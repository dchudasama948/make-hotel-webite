Lake View Suite

About This Room

A Room With A Story

The Lake View Suite is our most romantic offering — a spacious sanctuary that opens to panoramic views of the shimmering Lake Pichola. Mornings here begin with golden light dancing on the water, and evenings are painted in hues of amber and rose.

The suite spans 850 sq. ft. with a dedicated king bedroom, a sun-drenched living area, and a marble en-suite bathroom with a freestanding Jacuzzi tub positioned to capture the lake view. The private balcony is the jewel — perfect for quiet breakfasts or champagne sunsets.

Every detail has been curated: hand-block-printed cushions, local artisan woodwork, and a bespoke scent crafted exclusively for The Grand Lotus.
