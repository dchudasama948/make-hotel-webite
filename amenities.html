Premium Facilities

Everything You Need

From wellness and dining to recreation and business, our curated amenities ensure your stay exceeds every expectation.

Wellness & Recreation

🏊

Infinity Pool

Our rooftop infinity pool overlooks the shimmering Lake Pichola. Open daily 7 AM – 10 PM. Poolside bar and loungers available.

💆

Ayurveda Spa

Indulge in traditional Rajasthani healing therapies, deep tissue massages, and signature Lotus Beauty treatments.

🏋

Fitness Centre

State-of-the-art equipment, personal trainers on request. Open 24 hours. Yoga sessions available on the terrace every morning.

🧘

Yoga & Meditation

Daily sunrise yoga on our garden terrace led by certified instructors. Private sessions available on request.

🎾

Outdoor Activities

Boat rides on Lake Pichola, sightseeing tours, cycling, and curated cultural excursions to Udaipur's heritage sites.

♟

Recreation Lounge

Indoor games room with chess, carrom, and board games. Cozy reading library with curated literature on Rajasthan.

Dining & Beverages

🍽

Rooftop Restaurant

Multi-cuisine fine dining with sweeping city and lake views. Serving Indian, Continental, and Rajasthani cuisine with live folk music.

☕

The Lotus Café

All-day café serving artisan coffees, herbal teas, and light snacks. Perfect for a casual breakfast or afternoon retreat.

🍸

Heritage Bar

Curated cocktails, fine wines, premium spirits, and local beverages. The perfect setting for sunset evenings.

🛎

24/7 Room Service

Round-the-clock in-room dining with an extensive menu. Private dining setup available for romantic occasions.

🥗

Breakfast Buffet

Lavish spread of Indian and continental breakfast options daily from 7 AM to 11 AM. Included in select room packages.

🎂

Celebration Packages

Custom cake, floral arrangements, and special dinner setups for anniversaries, birthdays, and proposals.

Services & Business

📶

High-Speed WiFi

Complimentary high-speed internet throughout the property — rooms, restaurant, pool, and lobby.

🚗

Valet Parking

Complimentary valet and secured parking for all guests. Airport and railway station transfers on request.

💼

Business Centre

Conference hall with AV equipment, high-speed internet, printing, and secretarial services for corporate guests.

🎉

Banquet & Events

Grand event halls for weddings, conferences, and private parties. Capacity up to 500 guests. In-house event management team.

👶

Childcare Services

Supervised kids' activity room and babysitting services available on prior request to ensure a peaceful stay for parents.

🧳

Concierge & Tours

Expert concierge team available 24/7. City sightseeing tours, cultural experiences, and heritage walks arranged daily.
